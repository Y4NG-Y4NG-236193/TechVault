# Branding Guidelines: "The Vault"

The "The Vault" branding is an industrial minimalist design system created for TechVault-Admin. It emphasizes precision, high contrast, and structural clarity, moving away from generic "vibe-coded" aesthetics.

## 🎨 Color Palette

### Primary Colors (60-30-10 Rule)
- **Base (60%)**: `#09090b` (Carbon) - Used for major backgrounds.
- **Surface (30%)**: `#18181b` (Surface) - Used for borders, cards, and secondary backgrounds.
- **Accent (10%)**: `#bef264` (Cyber Lime) - Used sparingly for active states, highlights, and primary icons.

### Support Colors
- **Foreground**: `#fafafa` (Pure White) - Used for high-contrast primary text.
- **Muted Text**: `zinc-600` / `zinc-500` - Used for secondary labels and headers.
- **Critical**: `rose-500` - Used for destructive actions (e.g., Logout).

## 🔠 Typography

### Brand Logo
- **Font**: Sans-serif (Geist/Inter)
- **Weight**: Black (900)
- **Tracking**: `-0.05em` (tighter)
- **Case**: Uppercase
- **Aesthetic**: Bold, industrial, and solid.

### Group Headers
- **Size**: 10px / 0.625rem
- **Weight**: Bold (700)
- **Tracking**: `0.2em` (widest)
- **Case**: Uppercase
- **Aesthetic**: Structured and organizational.

### Navigation Labels
- **Size**: 14px / 0.875rem
- **Weight**: Medium (500)
- **Aesthetic**: Clean and functional.

## 📐 Layout & Interaction

### Border Strategy
- Use sharp, 1px lines (`border-[#18181b]`) for separation.
- Avoid heavy shadows or large corner radii.
- Focus on clean, geometric shapes.

### Active States
- Indicated by a 2px left border (`border-l-2`) in the accent color (**Cyber Lime**).
- Accompanied by a surface background change to distinguish the active route.

### Iconography
- **Weight**: Standard (Lucide default)
- **Size**: 20px (h-5/w-5) for main navigation.
- **Style**: Line icons, occasionally filled in the logo or active states.
